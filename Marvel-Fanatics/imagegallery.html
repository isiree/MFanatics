Captain America

Captain America is a superhero in the Marvel Comics universe, created by Joe Simon and Jack Kirby in 1941. His real name is Steve Rogers, a frail man who was transformed into a super-soldier during World War II. With his iconic shield and unwavering sense of justice, Captain America fights for truth, freedom, and the American way.

Hawkeye

Hawkeye is a skilled marksman and member of the Avengers in the Marvel Comics universe. His real name is Clint Barton, and he was trained by the Swordsman and Trickshot as a child. Hawkeye is often depicted as a rogue with a heart of gold, using his bow and arrows to fight for justice.

Iron Man

Iron Man is a Marvel superhero created by writer Stan Lee and designed by artist Don Heck and first appeared in 1963. The character, whose real name is Tony Stark, is a wealthy industrialist who invents a suit of powered armor to become a technologically advanced superhero. He is known for his wit, intelligence, and technological prowess.

Black Panther

Black Panther is a superhero in the Marvel Comics universe, created by writer Stan Lee and artist Jack Kirby in 1966. His real name is T'Challa, king and protector of the fictional African nation of Wakanda. He possesses superhuman strength, agility, and senses, as well as advanced technology and fighting skills, and is known for his iconic vibranium suit.

Black Widow

Black Widow, also known as Natasha Romanoff, is a highly trained spy and former assassin in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. She is a skilled hand-to-hand combatant, expert marksman, and has extensive training in various forms of espionage. She is a core member of the Avengers and a valued ally in the fight against evil.

Hulk

Hulk is a Marvel Comics character known for his incredible strength and his alter ego, Dr. Bruce Banner. When Banner is angered or stressed, he transforms into the Hulk, a massive green humanoid with seemingly limitless power. Despite his strength, Hulk is often portrayed as a tragic figure struggling to control his rage.

Thor

Thor, based on Norse mythology, is a Marvel superhero known for his godly powers and the mystical hammer Mjolnir. He is the prince of Asgard and a founding member of the Avengers. Thor possesses immense strength, durability, and lightning powers, making him a formidable force against his enemies, both on Earth and in the cosmos.
